INTRODUCTION

This system will rotate and place the solar panels (photovoltaic panels) to face the direction of direct sunlight to generate optimum levels of electricity throughout the day. The energy generation will be monitored from the system and the real-time data will be sent to the client with monthly, and weekly predictions. When the system is installed in the clientâ€™s premises, the location details will be gathered and the gathered data will be showcased on a public website for future clients to refer to and make decisions to switch to solar energy.

Main features of the the System

  • Rotating and moving the solar panel to face the direction of the sun
  • Energy generation monitoring
  • Real-time data presentation
  • Statistics presentation
  • Periodic data prediction
  • Location-Based Energy Mapping
  • User-Centric Interface : Enhancing Access and Control in Real-Time Systems

problem and solution

Problem

Solar Panel Efficiency Challenges

Stationary solar panels cannot provide maximum energy generation all day long, due to sun rays not falling on the surface providing maximum coverage.

Monitoring Gaps in Solar Systems

No method to get real-time data and and periodic predictions in existing solar panel solutions.

Financial Hurdles to Solar Adoption

Reluctancy of prospective customers to invest in solar panels.

problem and solution

solution

Optimizing Solar Panel Efficiency

Introduce the dual-axis rotation system designed to align solar panels with the sun for maximum energy generation.

Smart Energy Monitoring and Prediction

Highlight the solution of providing real-time energy status via a mobile app and predictive data, ensuring users stay informed about their solar panel performance.

Location-Based Energy Showcase Portal

Propose a portal for prospective customers, businesses, and marketing, leveraging location-based data to showcase energy generation and cost savings for informed investment decisions.
